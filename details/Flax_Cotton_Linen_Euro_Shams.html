Flax-Cotton Linen Euro Shams - Soft, Breathable, and Eco-Friendly

Soft, Breathable, and Eco-Friendly Linen Euro Shams

Indulge in the ultimate comfort with our Linen Euro Shams, crafted from a premium blend of flax and cotton. They offer the perfect balance of softness and breathability, making them ideal for a restful night’s sleep while also being gentle on your skin.

Each sham is made from an eco-friendly material, combining 55% linen and 45% cotton for a sustainable and luxurious feel. The breathable fabric works wonders in maintaining a cool and fresh sleeping environment, especially during warmer months.

Designed with an easy-to-clean envelope closure, these shams simplify maintenance, ensuring your bedding stays in pristine condition. Plus, they are machine washable at 60°C, making them incredibly convenient for everyday use.

With a size of 24'' x 24'', these decorative pillowcases are perfectly sized to complement your Euro bedding. Their undwickled fabric retains a natural drape and soft texture, offering a unique aesthetic that enhances any room's decor.

Whether you're staying at home or traveling, these shams are the perfect addition to your bedroom. They combine durability, style, and comfort, keeping your pillow fresh and looking great for years to come.

Upgrade your sleep experience with a product that values both your comfort and the planet. These Linen Euro Shams are a must-have for anyone who appreciates quality, sustainability, and effortless care.
